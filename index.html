Doing Some Vacationing

I love to vacation, but then again, who doesn't?


My First Vacation

I had my very first vacation around my 16th birthday. It was my first time taking an airplane and my first time out of the midwest..and I was headed to the infamous city of Los Angeles.

Next Few Vactions

The next several trips were back to LA on my 17th and again on my 18th birthdays. It was a popular place for my family to travel to during those times. I also took a couple of short trips to Chicago.

First Solo Trip

Being busy with work and school, I did not travel much until some time after I graduated college. This time, I was headed out of the country to Thailand...by myself.

Yellowstone

I have also visited Yellowstone a couple of times. It is quite a beautiful site!

On to the Next Adventure

The next year I found myself having the opportunity to travel to China.

Back Again

I went back to China again the next year.

Making it a Regular Thing

This month I am headed yet again to China to visit those I have come to know and see more of the country I have not yet had a chance to explore.
